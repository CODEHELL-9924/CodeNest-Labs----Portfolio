FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to the things most clients ask before we get started.

How long does a typical project take? +
Timelines depend on project scope. A simple website typically takes 2–4 weeks. Mobile apps and custom software range from 6–20 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline in every proposal before work begins.
Do you offer maintenance after launch? +
Yes — all plans include post-launch support. We also offer ongoing maintenance retainers for clients who want a long-term partner to handle updates, performance monitoring, and feature additions.
What technologies do you use? +
We work across React, Next.js, Vue, Flutter, React Native, Node.js, Laravel, Django, and more. We recommend the best technology for your specific project — not just what we're comfortable with.
Can you redesign or rebuild an existing system? +
Absolutely. We handle legacy system modernization, UI/UX redesigns, and full platform rebuilds regularly. We always start with a technical audit to understand what exists before proposing a path forward.
Do you provide hosting and domain services? +
We can manage the full hosting setup on AWS, Google Cloud, or your preferred provider. We also assist with domain registration, SSL, and deployment pipelines — everything to get you live and running smoothly.
What's your payment structure? +
We typically work on a milestone-based structure: an initial deposit to begin, payments at key milestones, and a final payment on delivery. This keeps both sides accountable and gives you full visibility into progress.
